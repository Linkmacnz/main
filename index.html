Instructions

  • Use Arrow Left and Arrow Right to move.
  • Press Space to jump.
  • Avoid moving drones (moving obstacles) and stationary spikes.
  • Collect coins for score. Background changes after score thresholds.
  • Lose all lives and you'll see the Game Over message — press Restart to try again.
